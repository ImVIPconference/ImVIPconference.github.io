IMVIP 2021 - September 2-3, 2021 will take place in Dublin City University, Ireland

August, 23 Update:
IMVIP 2021 will be conducted on-site at DCU Glasnevin campus, Stokes building, on September 2-3.

All presenters are invited to attend in person and present their work. Remote presentation can be facilitated on request, further details will be sent to authors.
Registration is now open.

We invite submissions to IMVIP 2021 of papers presenting novel research contributions or applications related to any aspect of computer vision or image processing. IMVIP is the annual conference of the Irish Pattern Recognition and Classification Society, a member body of the International Association for Pattern Recognition (IAPR).

Contributions are sought in all aspects of image processing, pattern analysis and machine vision, including but not restricted to the following topics:

Visually Guided Robot Manipulation and Navigation
Computer Vision for Autonomous Vehicles
Augmented Reality/Virtual Reality
Data Clustering and Texture Analysis
Image & Video Representation, Compression and Coding
Medical and Biomedical Imaging
Active Vision, Tracking and Motion Analysis
Object and Event Recognition
Face and Gesture Recognition
2D, 3D Scene Analysis and Visualisation
Deep Learning for Computer Vision
Image/Shape Representation and Recovery
Applications, Architectures and Systems Integration
Education in Image Processing or Machine Vision

Submission Details

Full Papers must be a maximum of 8 pages including references and appendices.

Short papers must be a maximum of 4 pages including references and appendices.

All papers will be presented shortly as long (15 mins) or short (10 mins) presentation.
